1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly. However, our games may use third-party services that collect information, which may be used to identify you.

Third-Party Services

Our games utilize Google Play Services, which may collect certain information to provide better services, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version)

You can learn more about Google's data collection and privacy practices by visiting Google's Privacy Policy .

2. How We Use Information

The information collected via Google Play Services is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the gameplay experience

3. Third-Party Service Providers

Our games integrate Google Play Services, which may collect data as outlined above. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your information:

Our games are intended for users of all ages, but we are particularly mindful of protecting the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. 8. Internet Connectivity

Our games require an active internet connection to access certain features, including:

  • Version Updates: To ensure optimal performance and security, our games may periodically check for and download updates over the internet.
  • Rate the Game: The option to rate the game via external platforms (e.g., Google Play) requires an internet connection and may direct you to a third-party service (such as the Google Play Store), subject to their own privacy policies.
  • Share Screenshots: If you choose to share in-game screenshots via social media or other platforms, the game will access the internet to upload or share this content through the selected platform. The shared content is subject to the privacy policies of the platform you use.
  • Send Feedback: If you choose to send feedback through the game, the feedback will be transmitted over the internet and sent to us. This feedback may include your device information, game version, and any comments or issues you report.

Please be aware that when using these internet-enabled features, certain data (such as device information, IP address, and game usage statistics) may be transmitted to our servers or third-party platforms.

9. Data Collection for Feedback and Support

When you submit feedback, we may collect the following information to improve our services and provide support:

  • Feedback Content: Any comments or issues you provide in feedback forms.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, such as the model, operating system, and app version, may be collected to help us resolve any reported issues.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you use the app (e.g., gameplay time, session data) to better understand any problems you encounter.

We use this information solely to improve the game and provide support. Feedback data is not shared with third parties, except when required by law.

10. Third-Party Platforms for Sharing and Rating

If you choose to rate the game or share in-game content (such as screenshots) via social media or other external platforms, the data you share will be subject to the privacy policies of those platforms. We do not control the data collection practices of these third-party platforms. Please review the privacy policies of those services before sharing content.

11. Security of Shared Content

When sharing screenshots or sending feedback through the game, we take reasonable precautions to secure your data during transmission. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data.

12. Consent to Use Internet for Feedback, Updates, and Sharing

By using the game's internet-enabled features (e.g., updates, rating, feedback, or screenshot sharing), you consent to the transmission of the relevant data over the internet. If you do not wish to share certain types of data, you may choose not to use these features.
